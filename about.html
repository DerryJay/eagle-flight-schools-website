About Eagle Flight Schools Agbani

Excellence in Secondary Education Since 2004

Our Mission

At Eagle Flight Schools Agbani, we are dedicated to providing quality secondary education that prepares students for successful futures in higher education and careers. Our mission is to deliver comprehensive, character-focused education that combines academic excellence with moral development and modern teaching methods.

We believe that every student deserves personalized attention, expert instruction, and access to modern learning facilities. Our commitment to excellence has made us a trusted name in secondary education in Enugu State for over 20 years.

Our Story

2004

Foundation

Eagle Flight Schools Agbani was founded with a vision to provide exceptional secondary education in Agbani, Enugu State, Nigeria, starting with basic facilities and dedicated teachers.

2008

Expansion

Added modern science laboratories and computer labs to enhance practical learning and technology education for our students.

2012

Recognition

Achieved outstanding WAEC results with 95% pass rate, establishing our reputation for academic excellence in Enugu State.

2016

Innovation

Integrated modern ICT facilities and digital learning platforms to prepare students for the digital age while maintaining strong academic foundations.

2024

Today

Now educating over 800 students annually with modern facilities and a team of 45 qualified teachers across all subjects.

Our Core Values

Character First

Character development is our top priority. We maintain the highest moral standards and instill strong values and integrity in all our students.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in everything we do, from academic instruction to student development, ensuring our students receive the best possible education.

Integrity

We conduct our operations with honesty, transparency, and ethical practices, building trust with our students, parents, and community.

Innovation

We embrace new technologies and teaching methods to enhance the learning experience while respecting traditional educational principles.
